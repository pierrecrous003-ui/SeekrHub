Where Solutions Architects Fit Within Organisations in United Arab Emirates

Insights into the Solutions Architect role in United Arab Emirates

Solutions Architects play an important role in how organisations operate and make decisions. In United Arab Emirates, professionals in this role work across a variety of industries and organisational environments.

This page provides an overview of how Solutions Architects typically function in United Arab Emirates, including the skills they apply and the contexts in which they work.

What Does a Solutions Architect Do?

A Solutions Architect applies specialised knowledge to support organisational objectives. While responsibilities vary, the role commonly involves analysis, collaboration, and domain-specific problem solving.

Typical Responsibilities

  • Applying role-specific expertise to organisational challenges
  • Collaborating with teams and stakeholders
  • Supporting operational or strategic objectives
  • Adapting responsibilities based on organisational needs

Industries Where Solutions Architects Work

  • Technology and digital services
  • Finance and professional services
  • Healthcare and life sciences
  • Manufacturing and operations
  • Public sector and government
