Scrum Masters play an important role in how organisations operate and make decisions. In Singapore, professionals in this role work across a variety of industries and organisational environments.

Scrum Masters in Singapore

In Singapore, Scrum Master roles exist across private, public, and hybrid organisations, with expectations shaped by local industry conditions.

Industries Where Scrum Masters Work

  • Technology and digital services
  • Finance and professional services

What Does a Scrum Master Do?

A Scrum Master applies specialised knowledge to support organisational objectives. While responsibilities vary, the role commonly involves analysis, collaboration, and domain-specific problem solving.
