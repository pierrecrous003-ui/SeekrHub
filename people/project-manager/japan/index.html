What Does a Project Manager Do?

A Project Manager applies professional expertise to support organisational goals through analysis, planning, collaboration, and the use of role-specific tools and methodologies.

This role is common in construction, technology, consulting, and enterprise transformation initiatives.

Common Skills and Competencies

  • Planning and coordinating project activities
  • Managing timelines, budgets, and stakeholders
  • Analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills
  • Adaptability in dynamic work environments
